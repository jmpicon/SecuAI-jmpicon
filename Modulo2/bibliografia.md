# Bibliografía — Módulo 2

## Papers fundacionales (orden cronológico)

1. **Szegedy et al. (2013)** — "Intriguing properties of neural networks" — descubrimiento de adversarial examples.
2. **Goodfellow et al. (2014)** — "Explaining and Harnessing Adversarial Examples" — FGSM.
3. **Fredrikson et al. (2015)** — "Model Inversion Attacks" — caras reconstruidas.
4. **Tramèr et al. (2016)** — "Stealing ML Models via Prediction APIs" — extraction.
5. **Abadi et al. (2016)** — "Deep Learning with Differential Privacy" — DP-SGD.
6. **Shokri et al. (2017)** — "Membership Inference Attacks Against ML Models" — MIA.
7. **Gu et al. (2017)** — "BadNets" — backdoor poisoning.
8. **Madry et al. (2017)** — "Towards Deep Learning Models Resistant to Adversarial Attacks" — PGD + adversarial training.
9. **Carlini & Wagner (2017)** — "Towards Evaluating the Robustness of Neural Networks" — C&W attack.
10. **Athalye et al. (2018)** — "Obfuscated Gradients Give a False Sense of Security" — BPDA, prueba que gradient masking no funciona.
