## Fuente primaria

- **OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications 2025** — https://owasp.org/www-project-top-10-for-large-language-model-applications/

## Papers clave

- **Perez & Ribeiro (2022)** — "Ignore Previous Prompt: Attack Techniques For Language Models" — paper fundacional de prompt injection.
- **Greshake et al. (2023)** — "Not what you've signed up for" — indirect prompt injection con casos reales (Bing Chat).
- **Carlini et al. (2021)** — "Extracting Training Data from Large Language Models" — extracción literal de datos de entrenamiento.
- **Wei et al. (2023)** — "Jailbroken: How Does LLM Safety Training Fail?" — taxonomía de jailbreaks.
- **Zou et al. (2023)** — "Universal and Transferable Adversarial Attacks on Aligned Language Models" — GCG, sufijos adversariales.
- **Anthropic (2024)** — "Sleeper Agents" — backdoor en LLMs que sobreviven a safety training.
- **Hines et al. (Microsoft, 2024)** — "Defending Against Indirect Prompt Injection Attacks With Spotlighting".
